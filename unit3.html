Unit 3
Boolean Expressions
and If Statements
Students deepen their understanding of boolean logic by building a traffic simulation where animated cars respond to a working traffic light, with speed controlled by mouse position and user-defined events. Alongside the project, students tackled CodingBat string problems, comparing multiple algorithmic approaches and stress-testing solutions against edge cases. A pedestrian button introduced compound boolean conditions, giving students a concrete reason to investigate De Morgan's Law and practice rewriting complex expressions into equivalent, more readable forms.
