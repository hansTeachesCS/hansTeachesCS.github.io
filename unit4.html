Unit 4
Iteration
Students explore loops by building toward a personal identity flag, a remix of the US flag that incorporates imagery meaningful to them, with the only hard requirement being exactly 50 stars placed using loops in any arrangement they choose. Along the way, they trace through for loops and while loops, convert between the two, and tackle nested loops to generate grids, pyramids, and other patterns. CodingBat string problems run alongside the project, pushing students to think algorithmically about iteration, accumulation, and early returns.
